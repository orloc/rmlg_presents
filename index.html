Some History

how we got here

We were a small industrial and trade focused corporation founded by old friends and revitalized by new players.

We moved from high sec early and grew quickly in our new home. Since that initial arudous journey we have matured into a formidable group who not only have continued to pursue our industrial interests while not losing sight of the space we must defend in order to do so.

Over the past 6 months we have grown from a small mostly inexperienced group of strangers to a large contributor to one of the largest ( and more progressive ) alliances in the whole of New Eden. I want to thank and congratulate all of those who have been with us from the beginning - and welcome all the people just joining for the ride as we all have great hopes for the things to come.
- management

Not Taxes - dues

Each member will pay for their alliance contribution AND corporation taxes via a single corporation issued contract. Taxes are calculated by the number of ACCOUNTS a member uses.

  • Contracts will be issued to each member monthly
  • Tax Formula (per character)= BASE_RATE / ( 1 + PERCENT_FALLOFF )N
    where N is the account number ( 1 , 2 , 3 etc ).
  • The totals are then summed and you are issued a bill.
  • Examples:
    1. 100m
    2. 180m
    3. 244m
    4. 295m
    5. etc..

By and large everyone should SAVE money - based on the contributions we recieved and the suggested alliance contribution of 4 hours these numbers seem to work out.

We have plans to add reductions and "penalties" in the future to create incentives toward acoomplishing corporation and alliance initiatives.
